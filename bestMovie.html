Why I Like This Movie

This is my favorite movie because it was fairly accurate in bringing marvels comic book world to the screen. It is exciting and continues Captain Americas mission to save the world. There are cliffhangers, drama, and exciting scenes.

Favorite Actor

My favorite actor in Civil War is Sebastian Stan because he is absolutly fantastic and very dramatic, being able to convey his emotions without many speaking lines.
